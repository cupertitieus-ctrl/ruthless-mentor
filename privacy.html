Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

The short version: You own your manuscript. We only collect what we need to deliver your review and run the service. We never sell your data.

1. Who We Are

Ruthless Mentor ("we," "us," "our") operates the website at ruthlessmentor.com, which provides honest manuscript reviews to authors. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. What We Collect

Information you give us directly

  • Email address — required when you submit a manuscript for review, so we can send you your report.
  • Manuscript text — the writing you paste into our review form. Stored so your review can be retrieved later via your personal dashboard or a shareable link.
  • Manuscript metadata — the genre, stage, POV, title, and other details you select in the review form.
  • Account information — if you create an account, we store your email and an encrypted password (handled by our auth provider, Supabase).
  • Contact form submissions — messages you send through our contact page.

Payment information

We use Stripe to process payments. We never see or store your credit card details. Stripe handles all payment data according to their own privacy policy and PCI compliance standards.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data via Google Analytics (pages visited, referring site, browser type, approximate location) to understand how the site is used.
  • Essential cookies for logged-in sessions (so you stay signed in).

3. What We Do With It

We use your information only to:

  • Generate your manuscript review and send it to you
  • Let you access past reviews via your dashboard and shareable links
  • Process your payment
  • Respond to your contact form submissions and support requests
  • Understand how the site is performing (aggregated analytics)

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to anyone, ever.
  • Claim any ownership of your manuscript. You own your work.
  • Share your manuscript with anyone outside the services required to deliver your review.
  • Send marketing emails unless you explicitly opt in.

4. Your Manuscript

When you submit a manuscript for review, the text is stored in our database so you can access the review later via your dashboard or a shareable link. The original manuscript text may also be stored temporarily to recover from failed reviews. You can request deletion of any stored manuscript at any time by emailing support@ruthlessmentor.com.

5. Service Providers

We use trusted service providers to run this site. We do not sell your data or share it for advertising.

  • Stripe — processes your payment. Handles all card data securely.
  • Supabase — stores your review, account info, and session data.
  • Resend — sends your review delivery email and other transactional messages.
  • Render — hosts the website and its backend.
  • Google Analytics — aggregate usage data for site improvement.

6. How Long We Keep Data

We keep your review and account data as long as your account is active or until you request deletion. You can request removal of any of your data at any time by emailing support@ruthlessmentor.com.

Contact form submissions are kept only as long as needed to respond to your message.

7. Your Rights

Regardless of where you live, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of any non-essential communications

To exercise any of these rights, email support@ruthlessmentor.com and we'll respond within 30 days.

For California residents (CCPA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion, and opt out of the sale of your information. We do not sell your personal information. To exercise your CCPA rights, email us at the address above.

For EU/UK residents (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation, you have the rights listed above, plus the right to withdraw consent, object to processing, and lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. Our legal basis for processing your data is performance of a contract (delivering your review) and your consent (for optional analytics).

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for adult authors. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, email us and we will delete it immediately.

9. Security

We use industry-standard security practices including HTTPS encryption, hashed passwords, secure API tokens, and row-level security on our database. No system is 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect your data.

10. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top and notify you by email if the changes are significant. Continued use of the service after an update means you accept the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

Questions about this privacy policy, your data, or anything related to your privacy? Email us at support@ruthlessmentor.com. We'll respond within 48 hours.
