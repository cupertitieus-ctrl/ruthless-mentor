Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

The short version: You own your manuscript. We only use what you share with us to deliver your review. We never sell your data, and we never claim any rights to your writing.

WHO WE ARE

Ruthless Mentor operates the website at ruthlessmentor.com, which provides honest manuscript reviews to authors.

YOUR MANUSCRIPT

You own your manuscript. Full stop. Submitting it for review does not transfer any rights or ownership to us. We do not republish, resell, or distribute your writing. Your review is stored so you can access it later via a shareable link or your account dashboard. You can request deletion of any stored manuscript at any time through our contact page.

INFORMATION COLLECTION AND USE

Ruthless Mentor may collect both personally identifiable and non-personally identifiable information (known as "personal data" and "non-personal data" outside of the US, and both referred to as "Personal Information" within this policy) from our users (such as names, email addresses, etc.). We use this information to fill orders promptly, process payments for services, provide customer service assistance, improve the review experience, and improve the content we provide to better meet our users' needs. This information is never sold or rented to outside parties.

We may collect the following data if relevant:

  • Your name.
  • Your email address.
  • Any username used to identify yourself in our services.
  • Your IP address.
  • Non-personally identifiable technical details about any device you use to access our services, including your internet or network connection, operating system, browser type, or other software.
  • Details of your use of our services, including metrics data about when and how you use the services.
  • The manuscript text and metadata you submit for review.
  • Any other data which you supply us via our services.

Your data may be used for the following purposes:

  • To carry out our obligations arising from any agreements between you and us.
  • To provide you with the review and related services you request from us.
  • To inform you of updates or changes to our services.
  • To maintain, improve, or modify our services.
  • For tax, legal, and accounting purposes.
  • For accountability purposes as defined by EU legislation (GDPR).

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to anyone, ever.
  • Claim any ownership of your manuscript. You own your work.
  • Share your manuscript with anyone outside the services required to deliver your review.
  • Send marketing emails unless you explicitly opt in.

PAYMENT INFORMATION

Payments are processed by a secure third-party payment processor. We never see or store your credit card details.

THIRD PARTY INFORMATION COLLECTION AND EXTERNAL SERVICES

Some Ruthless Mentor services, including blog content and shared review links, may contain links to and from the websites or services of third parties. Our Privacy Policy does not extend to these external sites or companies, so please refer directly to their privacy policies.

TRUSTED PARTNERS

Ruthless Mentor, as the service provider, is the controller in respect of personal data that it processes in the provision of the Services for the purposes of the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679, or any applicable national implementing laws, regulations and secondary legislation implementing the requirements of such regulation (the "GDPR").

We may share your data with Trusted Partners, formally referred to as "Data Processors," who are engaged by us to help deliver our services and functionalities to you. We always provide our partners with the minimum data necessary for them to achieve the purpose of their cooperation with us, but you acknowledge that the following may have access to limited data about you and process it on our behalf:

  • Our partners who provide us with internal management and data-sharing tools;
  • Our partners who help us in data analysis by providing us with analytical tools;
  • Our partners who help us manage our newsletters and email communications by providing us with email marketing tools;
  • Our partners who assist us with development, maintenance, and support of the Ruthless Mentor service platform;
  • Our partners who assist us in the marketing and advertising of Ruthless Mentor services;
  • Our professional advisors who assist with legal, tax, audit, or accounting matters.

Note that your data will only be made available to Trusted Partners for the purpose of pursuing legitimate interests of Ruthless Mentor and provision of its services. Your personal information is never sold or traded with third parties for marketing purposes.

DISCLOSURE OF INFORMATION

Ruthless Mentor reserves the right to disclose any content, records, or electronic communication of any kind:

  • (i) To satisfy any legal request;
  • (ii) If such disclosure is necessary or appropriate to enforce license or service agreements; or
  • (iii) To protect the rights of Ruthless Mentor or its users or partners.

WILL RUTHLESS MENTOR SEND ME EMAILS?

We may contact you with information about product announcements, software updates, and special offers. We consider your acceptance of this Policy as your acceptance of our offer to send you these emails. You may opt out of such communications at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link found within any Ruthless Mentor email.

YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or export any personal information we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, reach out through our contact page and we'll respond promptly.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our service is intended for adult authors. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
