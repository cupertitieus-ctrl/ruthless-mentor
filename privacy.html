Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

The short version: You own your manuscript. We only use what you share with us to deliver your review. We never sell your data, and we never claim any rights to your writing.

WHO WE ARE

Ruthless Mentor operates the website at ruthlessmentor.com, which provides honest manuscript reviews to authors.

YOUR MANUSCRIPT

You own your manuscript. Full stop. Submitting it for review does not transfer any rights or ownership to us. We do not republish, resell, or distribute your writing. Your review is stored so you can access it later via a shareable link or your account dashboard. You can request deletion of any stored manuscript at any time through our contact page.

WHAT WE COLLECT AND WHY

To run this site and deliver your review, we gather a small set of information from you. That includes basic identifiers like your name and email, any username you use on the site, your IP address, and general technical information about the device and browser you're using. We also keep track of how you interact with the site so we can improve it over time. And of course, we store the manuscript text and any details you provide along with it when you submit a review.

Everything we collect is used for a simple list of purposes: delivering the services you asked us for, keeping our agreements with you, letting you know about updates or changes to the site, improving how the product works, and handling normal business obligations like tax and accounting. Nothing else. Your information is never sold, rented, or traded.

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to anyone, ever.
  • Claim any ownership of your manuscript. You own your work.
  • Share your manuscript with anyone outside the services required to deliver your review.
  • Send marketing emails unless you explicitly opt in.

PAYMENT INFORMATION

Payments are processed by a secure third-party payment processor. We never see or store your credit card details.

LINKS TO OTHER WEBSITES

Some pages on this site may link out to other companies and services. Once you leave our site, this policy no longer applies. If you follow a link somewhere else, take a moment to read the privacy practices of whichever site you end up on.

SERVICE PROVIDERS WE WORK WITH

Running a website takes more than one company, so we rely on a handful of outside vendors to keep everything working. These are the kinds of services that might handle limited pieces of your information on our behalf:

  • Hosting and technical infrastructure that keeps the site online.
  • Tools that help us understand how the site is being used in aggregate.
  • Services that handle transactional emails, like delivering your review.
  • Professional advisors for legal, tax, or accounting matters when needed.

Any vendor we share information with only gets what they need to do their job, and never for their own marketing. We don't share your data to help anyone else advertise to you.

WHEN WE MIGHT SHARE INFORMATION

In rare situations, we may need to share information from your account or your use of the site. Those situations are limited to: responding to a valid legal request, enforcing our agreements with users, or protecting the rights and safety of Ruthless Mentor, our users, or the public. Outside of those cases, we keep your information to ourselves.

WILL RUTHLESS MENTOR SEND ME EMAILS?

From time to time we may send you updates about new features, product changes, or occasional promotions. If you'd rather not hear from us, every email we send includes an unsubscribe link at the bottom — one click and you're out.

YOUR RIGHTS

You can ask us to show you what information we have about you, correct anything that's wrong, delete your data, or hand it over in a portable format. Just reach out through our contact page and we'll take care of it.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our service is intended for adult authors. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

WILL THIS POLICY CHANGE?

We may update this policy from time to time as the site evolves. When we do, we'll change the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. If the changes are significant, we'll try to let you know directly — but we recommend checking in once in a while to see where things stand. Continuing to use the site after an update means you're on board with the new version. If you're not, you can always close your account.

CONTACT

Questions about this privacy policy or your data? Reach out through our contact page.
