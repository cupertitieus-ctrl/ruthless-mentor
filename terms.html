Ruthless Mentor ("we," "us," "our") provides honest manuscript reviews to authors through the website at ruthlessmentor.com. By using the service, you agree to these terms. If you don't agree, don't use the service.

2. What You Own

You own your manuscript. Full stop. Submitting a manuscript for review does not transfer any rights, ownership, or licensing to us. We do not claim authorship, co-authorship, or any creative rights over your writing. The review we provide is our commentary on your work — it does not make your book ours.

We will never use your manuscript to train AI models. We will never resell, republish, or distribute your writing. Your work is yours.

3. What You're Paying For

When you purchase a review, you're paying for a one-time manuscript evaluation delivered as a written report. The report includes first impressions, structure analysis, voice evaluation, prose quality, character feedback, line-level callouts, market reality assessment, and a final verdict — as described on the homepage.

The review is not a substitute for a developmental editor, copy editor, or beta reader. It's a ruthless, honest assessment meant to help you revise your work.

4. What You're Responsible For

  • Submitting writing that is yours (or that you have permission to submit)
  • Not submitting illegal, abusive, or infringing content

  • Paying the listed price (or using a valid coupon)
  • Reading the review with an open mind — we don't sugarcoat, and that's the whole point

5. What We're Responsible For

  • Delivering your review in a reasonable amount of time (usually within minutes)
  • Emailing you a link to your review report
  • Keeping your review accessible via shareable link and (if you have an account) your dashboard
  • Protecting your manuscript and account data as described in our Privacy Policy
  • Making it right if the review fails to deliver or is broken in a meaningful way

6. Refund Policy

If your review fails to generate, comes through incomplete, or contains a clear technical error, email support@ruthlessmentor.com and we will either regenerate the review for free or refund your payment in full.

We don't issue refunds because you disagree with the feedback. The reviews are designed to be ruthlessly honest, and honest feedback sometimes stings. That's the product. If you wanted cheerful encouragement, there are other places to get that.

Coupon-based free reviews are not eligible for refunds (there's nothing to refund).

7. Subscriptions

If you purchase a subscription plan, you are charged monthly until you cancel. You can cancel at any time from your dashboard. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period — you keep access to your remaining reviews until then. Unused reviews do not roll over to the next month.

You can use the service without an account (anonymous reviews are emailed to you as a shareable link). If you create an account, you're responsible for keeping your login credentials secure. You can delete your account at any time by emailing us.
